Description

This is a first look at our design and tech demo developed and created for you to experience ORB.

ORB is our proprietary Orbital Reconnaissance Bot, a friendly level one AI designed to serve as a companion in your adventures and quests through Cyberspace and the AR/MR/VR world.


*This is an excerpt from Cyberspace Station VR*

Features

 Simulation

  Log into the virtual space and evaluate your ORBs, by clicking, dragging, rotating, and laughing.

 Experience

  Customize ORB to your liking (ver. 02 will allow you to train your ORB with command prompts).

 Learn

  Engage your ORB's dialogue window to learn more of the backstory, creation and foundation.
