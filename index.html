FACT SHEET

Description

ORB stands for Orbital Reconnaisance Bot.

ORB is an Orbital Reconnaissance Bot, a friendly AGI, designed at Paul Arthur's lab within WE ARE of Earth to serve as a companion in your adventures and quests through Cyberspace and the AR/MR world.

ORBs are programmed and trained for data recording / data retrieval, and realtime reconnaissance.

In this simulation you will get to view and evaluate a prototype ORB and apply some virtual customization.
Good luck, Space Cowboy!

Features

 Simulation:

   Log into the virtual space and evaluate your ORBs functions, by clicking, dragging, rotating, and laughing.

 Experience:

   Customize your ORB to your liking (ver. 02 will allow you to train your ORB with command prompts).

 Learn:

   Engage your ORB's dialogue window to learn more of the backstory, creation and foudation.
